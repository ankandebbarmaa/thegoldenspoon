Our Heritage

The Golden Spoon brings the vibrant soul of Indian cuisine to your plate. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, perfected by our master chefs from Old Delhi to Kerala.


We use locally sourced ingredients blended with imported authentic spices to create rich curries, tandoori specialties, and delicate biryanis. We believe in "Atithi Devo Bhava" - The guest is equivalent to God.
